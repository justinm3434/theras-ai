The world optimizes for training.
We optimize for everything after.

Today's AI infrastructure treats inference as an afterthought — running trillion-parameter models on general-purpose hardware designed for flexibility, not speed. But once a model is trained, its weights are fixed. Why run them on hardware that doesn't know that?

Theras AI encodes model parameters directly into the physical substrate — weights become part of the chip itself. The result: inference that's orders of magnitude faster, cheaper, and more energy-efficient. No memory bottlenecks. No weight loading. Just permanent, instantaneous computation.

How It Works

⚡

Hard-Wired Weights

Model parameters are encoded into the final metal layers during fabrication. No DRAM reads. No memory bandwidth wall. Weights are the chip.

🔥

Inference-Native Silicon

Purpose-built for the forward pass. Every transistor is dedicated to inference — no wasted silicon on training flexibility you'll never use.

🌍

Sustainable Scale

Dramatically lower power consumption per inference. As AI scales to billions of users, the energy equation has to change. We're changing it.
